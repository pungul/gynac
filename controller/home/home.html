Ultrasound in Gynecology: An Atlas and Guide

WELCOME TO GYNECOLOGY ACADEMY


We are excited to bring you, a unique, user-friendly and comprehensive online learning portal for skill enhancement and training in the field of ultrasound imaging in Gynecology. Online learning is a convenient and self-paced way of acquiring specific knowledge and expertise in gynecological ultrasound, at the convenience of your own time and place.


If you are a practicing radiologist, gynecologist, sonologist or a medical student, in any country of the world, looking to build your knowledge in the field of Gynecological Ultrasound then you have come to the right place. The lectures and courses are open to candidates from all countries.


Our expert faculty with vast experience through their engaging lectures and interactive courses will provide you with an in-depth view of the subject. The courses are supported with abundant illustrations of classical and new ultrasound features in various pathologies including, not only two-dimensional grey scale but Doppler and three-dimensional ultrasound as well.


In order to give you a holistic experience and to help you easily understand the techniques of ultrasound imaging, we have incorporated video clips that capture the unique interactive nature of transvaginal scanning.


For more information please visit our About GynAc page. If you have any questions or comments, you are welcome to write to us at support@gynac.org
