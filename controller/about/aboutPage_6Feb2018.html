About Gynecology Academy

Gynecological ultrasound has seen rapid advances owing to an improvement in ultrasound technology, equipment, and expanding research. However, the literature in this niche area of medicine is still limited and scattered. Gynecological ultrasound has been in the shadow of obstetric and fetal medicine and there are very few courses that focus on training clinicians, radiologists and students on this subject. The objective of this portal is to bridge these gaps and provide comprehensive coverage, including recent advances and new-found knowledge in the field of gynecological ultrasound.


Gynecology Academy offers training through a flexible online program which is a convenient way of acquiring specific knowledge and expertise in gynecological ultrasound and can be done at your own pace, time and place. Candidates from all over the globe, especially practicing radiologists, gynecologists, sonologists and medical students with varying level of experience will greatly benefit from the courses we offer. Our course cover all topics in gynecological ultrasound including infertility and includes basic as well as advanced training on the subject.


We have done our best to develop an interesting, interactive and engaging course. The experienced faculty have put together their many years of diligent study of various cases with confirmed pathologies, including rare and challenging ones, into this teaching program, through their lectures which abound in images and videos.


Today, ultrasound examination is expected to provide accurate, prompt and site-specific diagnosis of gynecological pathology and Gynecology Academy will facilitate you to do that and in turn help scores of women in the timely diagnosis and treatment of their gynecological conditions.
