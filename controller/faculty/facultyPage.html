6 Month Certification Course

Faculty

Dr. Mala Sibal

Email: mala@gynac.org

Dr. Mala Sibal is a consultant physician at the Department of Fetal Medicine and Obstetric & Gynecological Ultrasound, Manipal Hospital, Bangalore. Dr. Sibal is also the founder of Gynecology Academy (www.gynac.org), an online learning portal for basic and advanced training in gynecological ultrasound.


She is the author of the book "Ultrasound in Gynecology: An Atlas and Guide" published by Springer in Nov 2016. The book provides a comprehensive coverage of diagnoses across a wide range of gynecological pathologies using various ultrasound techniques.


She has fourteen years of experience in the field of gynecological ultrasound, prior to which she practiced clinical obstetrics and gynecology for seven years. She has also trained and taught several practitioners and medical students in the field of gynecological ultrasound.


Dr. Sibal has been an invited speaker at numerous national and international conferences on this subject and has authored peer-reviewed journal articles on novel diagnostic ultrasound techniques in the American Journal of Ultrasound in Medicine. She is also a principal investigator in the ongoing International Ovarian Tumor Analysis (IOTA) International Research Program.


She holds a DNB post-graduate degree in Obstetrics and Gynecology and an MBBS graduate degree from Jawaharlal Nehru Medical College, Belgaum, where she stood first in Gynecology.

Dr. Sonal Panchal

Email: sonal@gynac.org

Dr. Sonal Panchal is a specialist in the areas of Infertility, Gynecology and high-risk Obstetric scans using Doppler and 3D-4D ultrasound.


She is a Consultant Sonologist at Dr. Nagori’s Institute for Infertility and IVF, Ahmedabad. Dr. Nagori’s Institute has been awarded the status of a Centre of Excellence by the Ian Donald Inter-University School of Medical Ultrasound under her directorship.


She is also a professor at Dubrovnik International University, Croatia, and is the National Academic Director of Ian Donald School, India where she also offers Master’s Courses in Ultrasound in ObGyn and Human Reproduction.


She has conducted original research work on follicular monitoring, endometrial receptivity and polycystic ovarian syndrome. She has also contributed to, co-authored and edited several books.


She has also published articles in several journals and has presented several papers at national and international conferences. She is a member of the National Advisory Board for the International Journal of Infertility and Fetal Medicine (Jaypee publication) and serves as a reviewer for several journals.


She has been honoured with Diploma of Appreciation from the Ian Donald Inter-University School of Medical Ultrasound, 2015, for training the maximum number of fellowship students.
