ULTRASOUND IN GYNECOLOGY

Four Month Certification Course

Gynecology Academy is pleased to announce a 4-month certification course:Ultrasound in Gynecology.

The course is open to candidates from all countries.

The online course can be initiated from any date according to your convenience and will last for a duration of 4 months from the date of your commencement.

Details of the course are as follows:

COURSE FACULTY: Dr Mala Sibal & Dr Sonal Panchal

The faculty are experts in the field of gynecological ultrasound. They have authored research articles and books on the subject and are passionate teachers and have trained several doctors in Gynecological Ultrasound for well over a decade. Click here for more details.

COURSE STRUCTURE:

The 1st course is scheduled from September 2nd to December 31st, 2017.

The subject matter has been sub-divided into modules which will be covered in 4 sessions.

The course may be taken either online or onsite.

Online Course: Every month a new session, containing 3 modules, Online learning is a convenient and self-paced way of acquiring specific knowledge and expertise in gynecological ultrasound, at the convenience of your own time and place.

Onsite Course:: The course will be conducted in Bangalore, on one predefined weekend (Saturday & Sunday) each month, for the 4 months (i.e., 8 days of training). This will most likely be the 1st weekend of every month. Based on availability, live demonstration of a few cases will be conducted. In addition to this, those attending the onsite course will have access to the online lectures.

COURSE CONTENT: The subject matter has been divided into 12 modules and each module consists of multiple lectures. Click here to see the lectures and topics that will be covered.

SPECIAL FEATURES:

•   A comprehensive coverage of all topics in gynecological ultrasound including Infertility. This includes basic and advanced training in the subject. Various techniques of evaluation and rare pathologies have also been included.

•   The lectures include abundant images and numerous videos. Videos are essential because transvaginal scanning is a dynamic & interactive examination, that can only be captured and explained well with the help of videos.

•   Self-assessment at the end of each lecture to test knowledge acquired.

•   Answering questions and clarification of doubts on material provided, for both online and onsite courses.

•   Access to online lectures for an extended period of time to allow self-paced learning and revision of talks and videos for in-depth learning.

•   Online submission of images for assessment.

•   In addition, we also have an optional test at the end of the course to certify that the student has a sound understanding of all aspects of Gynecological Ultrasound. The test will be conducted in person, either in Bangalore, or in a nearby city, if there are sufficient candidates.

PROSPECTIVE AUDIENCE: Gynecologists, radiologists, sonologists and post-graduate medical students who are practicing ultrasound and are interested in learning or enhancing their knowledge in Gynecological Ultrasound.

CERTIFICATION: The certificate provided at the end of the course, by the Academy, will be of three types:

Certificate of Participation: On enrolling for the course.

Certificate of Completion: If the images have been submitted as specified and 75% of lectures have been attended (onsite delegates)/self-assessment done (online delegates).

Certificate of Competence: In addition to the above, if the candidate has passed the test at the end of the course.

COURSE FEE: Rs 80,000 + tax (GST)

We have a 10 % inaugural discount for the first 50 candidates.

The online candidates who desire to do the optional test at the end of the course so as to obtain the certificate of competence, will be charged an additional fee of Rs 5000.
