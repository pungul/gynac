ULTRASOUND IN GYNECOLOGY

6 Month Certification Course

INDIVIDUAL LECTURES AND MODULES

Registrants can choose a single lecture or module or a combination of the lectures/modules to suit their personal requirements. Please note: There is overlap of information between various topics and therefore there is a possibility that in a talk, it may be mentioned that a particular part/aspect within a topic is covered in another talk.

DURATION OF ACCESS:

Access will be available for one month for individual lectures and 3 months for individual modules.

SPECIAL FEATURES:   


A comprehensive coverage of all topics in gynecological ultrasound including Infertility from which one can choose. This includes basic and advanced training on the topic. Various techniques of evaluation and rare pathologies have also been included.

The lectures include abundant images and numerous videos. Videos are essential because transvaginal scanning is a dynamic & interactive examination, that can only be captured and explained well with the help of videos.

Self-assessment at the end of each lecture to test knowledge acquired.

Answering questions and clarification of doubts on material provided.

Access to online lectures for an extended period of time to allow self-paced learning and revision of talks and videos for in-depth learning.

Online submission of images for assessment when an entire module is chosen.

CERTIFICATION: The certificate provided by the Academy, will be of two types:

Certificate of Participation: On enrolling for the lecture.

Certificate of Completion: If the images have been submitted (for selected module) and self-assessment done (for selected lecture).
