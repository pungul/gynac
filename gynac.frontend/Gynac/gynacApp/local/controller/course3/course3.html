IOTA CERTIFICATION PROGRAM

The IOTA Certification program is for practitioners desiring to enhance their ultrasound skills in evaluation of adnexal masses. Adnexal masses are frequently encountered and are often a challenge to diagnose and manage. This program is focused on providing knowledge of internationally accepted standardized terminology and methodologies for evaluation of adnexal masses. This will significantly help in determining whether these masses are benign or malignant, and the likely pathology. Dr Mala Sibal has been authorised to conduct IOTA Certification Course & Exams in India & Asia – by the IOTA group. A test will be conducted at the end of the program. On passing the IOTA test, you will be certified by the IOTA group (Europe). A certificate will be provided by them and your name will be uploaded on the IOTA website. Please visit www.iotagroup.org for details of the IOTA certifying body.

If you are interested in the IOTA course please register (registration is free) and mention so during the registration. We will keep you posted whenever an IOTA course is being conducted. Societies interested in conducting these courses can also get in touch with us.

Details of dates & locations will be available at www.gynac.org (this website) & Gynecology Academy facebook page. Stay tuned!
